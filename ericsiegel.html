Eric Siegel

By the age of 14, Eric Siegel had built his tv whilst also studying at Brooklyn's Samuel Gompers Vocational and Technical High School. In 1970, he developed the Siegel Colorizer and a video synthesizer. In 1969, the Howard Wise Gallery presenting the ground-breaking exhibition Television as a Creative Medium, that showcased his Psychedelavision in Color. Siegel, says that: "Psychedelavision is my attempt at video mind expansion. It is necessary to develop a new science that can penetrate human nature. Television will be one of the most crucial tools in this new science. The American Dream has ceased to change. It's degenerating right now. Television has to be freed."

Mark Amerika

Mark Amerika, who is regarded as one of the foremost pioneers of net art, mashes up popular media into fresh forms of digital art, cinema, projections, performance, installations, and texts. Amerika examines the manner in which individual identities navigate various spaces as it looks at the fuzziness between online and offline reality. According to Amerika, "what my collection of work actually investigates throughout time is the way digital media promotes the remixing of persona."

His work #NewAesthetic TV (Extended Play Remix) , which is the result of modifying source material from his transmedia production, examines what is usually referred to as glitch aesthetics. In order to create the Museum of Glitch Aesthetics (MOGA), 2012, real-time video feeds were manually edited to create what the United States refers to as "glitch aesthetics."
